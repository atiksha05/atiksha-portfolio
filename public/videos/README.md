# Background video

Your SF night timelapse:

- **`sf_night.mp4`** — already configured in `src/lib/data.ts`

## How it works

- Hero starts as a **solid black** background
- When you **move the mouse** or **touch the screen**, the timelapse video appears in a circle around your pointer
- Works on desktop (mouse) and phone (touch)

## Replace the video

Swap the file in this folder (keep the same name), or change the path in `src/lib/data.ts`.
